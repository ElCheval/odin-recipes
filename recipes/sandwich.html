Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Description

A go-to meal for lazy people, the ham and cheese sandwich is easy to make, packed with protein, relatively low in calories, and filling. Nothing is better when pressed for time, tired, or just in the mood for a good sandwich...

Ingredients

Makes one sandwich

  • Two slices of bread
  • One slice of your cheese of choice
  • Two slices of your deli meat of choice
  • 1 tbsp of some sort of spread (e.g., mayo, butter, cream cheese, etc...)

Steps

  1. Slather your slices of bread with your chosen spread
  2. Put cheese on first, later deli meat on one of the bread slices
  3. Toast or heat up on an oven
  4. Eat up!
